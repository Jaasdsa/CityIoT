6.验证录入数据正确性SQL

查询配置信息:

select  x.*,x1.ID  pointID ,x1.*  from( select t.ID stationID, t.GUID as stationCode,t.Name stationName,t1.ID sensorID,t1.Name sensorName,t1.PointAddressID from SCADA_Station t ,SCADA_Sensor t1 where t.ID=t1.StationID and t.ReadMode='WEB_PUMP_SCADA') x left join PointAddressEntry x1 on x.PointAddressID=x1.ID;
